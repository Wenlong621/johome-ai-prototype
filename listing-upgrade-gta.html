Modern new and spacious freehold townhome in Lawrence Heights offering an exceptional combination of natural light, privacy, and functional family living. Rarely found open views both front and back with no direct rear neighbours. Thoughtfully designed approx. 2,006 sq.ft. layout + spacious basement + private backyard + private garage; featuring 4 bedrooms and flexible den space, 4 bathrooms, and an open-concept main living area with 9 ft ceilings. Contemporary kitchen with quartz countertops, integrated pantry, glass tile backsplash, undercabinet lighting, stainless steel appliances. Spacious primary retreat features double walk-in closets and private ensuite. Detached garage through a private alley. Steps to subway station, parks, Yorkdale Mall, schools, shopping, and major highways.
